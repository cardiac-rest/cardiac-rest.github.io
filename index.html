¿Qué es "Rockbecca"?

What is "Rockbecca"?

Es una pregunta difícil de responder, pero la respuesta más apropiada sería "una carta de amor a los juegos de plataformas", o "el juego que me hubiera gustado encontrar hace 10 años".

It’s a hard question to answer, but the best one would be “a love letter to platforming games,” or “the game I wish I had found 10 years ago.”

En Cardiac Rest apreciamos fervientemente el legado que nos dejaron ciertas joyas...

At Cardiac Rest, we deeply value the legacy left by gems like the classic Sonic the Hedgehog series, the personality of Wario Land, and the positivity of Kirby games.

Es todo este conocimiento apasionado que nos ha llevado a lanzar Rockbecca...

This passion has led us to create Rockbecca, an adventure where we accompany Rebecca, the Stone Elemental, on her journey to discover who threatens the natural balance of the Elements.

Curiosidades

Trivia

Plataformas, transformaciones y un diseño EXTREMADAMENTE DURO.

Platforms, transformations, and EXTREMELY HARD design.

La historia de este juego surge en la sexta edición...

The idea began during the 6th Game Scholars Game Jam at URJC, with the theme “extremely hard.” That led us to the idea of a character turning into stone.

El diseño inicial se basaba en un juego con movimiento básico...

The first prototype had simple movement, wall-jumping, and the stone transformation. This became the foundation of our movement system.

Inicialmente, este juego comenzó como un proyecto con físicas...

Originally built in Unity with realistic physics, we switched to GameMaker Studio 2 due to time. Shockingly, we finished in time for the jam!

El juego consistía en lo que hoy es el primer nivel...

The jam version contained today’s first level (in parts) and a final boss fight against an early version of Valerie.
